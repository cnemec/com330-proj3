RPG MAKER HORROR GAMES

brief disclaimer

The horror game is definitely one of the hardest genres to get right, especially if the characters are small and the camera is zoomed out where everything is in frame. As such most of these games are not good, if anything most of them would make the player cringe before they induce fright.

Yume nikki inspiration

Some could argue that horror games in rpg maker are just inspired by yume nikki. While this is true the nightmares of yume nikki inspired creators to try to make these games scary I would say they aren't the same genre. While they came from the same roots they expanded into their own genres.

IB

IB is the story of a little girl, named IB, trapped in an art gallery, one that is slowly becoming hell pulling her in to teach her a lesson. Similar to silent hill in a way except obviously the little girl did nothing truly wrong to deserve being punished. Though now there the player has to run from monsters, solve puzzles and talk with other survivors to escape.

The story of IB is all about growing up, and how that causes people to see the world completely differently as they grow up. This can be seen in a few subtle ways only a game could show. Such as at the start of the game there are words that IB doesn't know but by the end if you read the text again she will understand the words. As well as seeing the game from the perspective of an adult and a child, things that would be cute and innocent for one of them will be dark and distorted for another, this has a real life parallel in porcelain dolls. There are many more examples of this but I will leave that to people curious in the games to discover themselves.
